Vaccine Twitter data analysis

Trends in the topics of US vaccine-related tweets
1/1/2020 - 4/18/2021

This website shows the trends in the topics discussed on Twitter around the COVID-19 vaccination. The tweets considered include the word vaccine or some of its variations (e.g. vaccination, vaccinate, vaccines, etc.) and were posted by users located across the US territory.
